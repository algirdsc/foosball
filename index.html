FOOTBALL ACADEMY

School aims to provide the highest levels of quality football training for young players, enhancing their football skills along with their social, emotional and physical health in a positive and safe environment. Built from mutual respect, character and hard work upon which every child can build success both on and off the field.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to develop confident players who are exciting and creative in their playing ability, balanced with the importance and understanding of how to incorporate their skills as part of a team. The keystone to successful players is positive attitude towards others, sportsmanship, and a willingness for improvement. SEQFA will strive to create and nurture a valuable football learning experience.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

We will create an environment where our players develop their skills and passion for the game of football which is emotionally and developmentally safe from harm. We will ensure our coaches are equipped with the skills and character required to maximize the learning outcomes, along with being good coaches, mentors and role models. Through a safe learning environment which is supportive to our players, we can strive to improve a player’s creative flair, adaptability for using individual and team play, and style of football built on a foundation of strong technical skills.

Success

Success must never be bench mark purely on results, especially from U8-U13 where players develop both physically and technically at much different rates (e.g. the most dominant player or team at U8 is seldom the most dominant at under 12).

Developing

In addition to developing technically sound players, we aim to develop our players both physically and psychologically, in terms of physical development. With a strong emphasis being placed on developing the players motor skills, which in modern times have unfortunately seen a rapid decline in today’s youth.

Attitude

To develop our players psychologically, we aim to teach our players valuable lessons though competition and prepare them for challenges they will face on the field and later in life. While we stress that “Winning” is not always important, we must emphasize that wanting to win and developing a winning attitude is important.

TECHNICAL

We teach players how to build up a range of movements and techniques related to the top-level professional soccer players. Coaching sessions have been designed to allow repetition of these fundamental actions in a fun and exciting way and to challenge and motivate players at all times.


TACTICAL

Enhance the players understanding of why, when and where the techniques are used in small-sided games leading up to 11 v 11 principles where relevant. Whatever their level SEQFA will help players to develop the courage and confidence to express their ability in games and an understanding of how to learn from their mistakes.
